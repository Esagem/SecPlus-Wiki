# Weak Areas

Accumulated misses from quizzes and sessions. The LLM appends rows here whenever you miss something during a study session.

The format is intentionally simple — date, concept, what you got wrong, what to revisit. Stale rows get retired once you nail the concept on a re-quiz.

## Convention

- One row per miss, even if multiple misses land on the same concept (so you see the recurrence pattern).
- "Revisit" should be a concrete next action: a concept page to read, a quiz bank to re-run, a transcript section to re-skim.
- If a concept shows up here 3+ times without resolution, escalate to a focused session.
